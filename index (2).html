About the school

Thomas Jefferson High School for Science & Technology

TJHSST — better known as TJ — is a magnet high school in Alexandria, Virginia, and consistently ranks as the #1 public high school in the United States. Admission is selective, competitive, and built around a rigorous entrance exam plus problem-solving essays.

  • Highly selective: Thousands apply, only a few hundred get in each year.
  • Top STEM curriculum: Research labs, advanced courses, and college-level projects.
  • National reputation: Ranked #1 public high school by U.S. News & World Report.
  • The test matters: Strong test prep is one of the biggest factors in admissions success.
